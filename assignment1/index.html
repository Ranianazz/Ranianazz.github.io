1 What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?

The first thing I noticed was the colour scheme on the screen. The website's creators used soft tones, including shades of blue and violet, and there was a number 2 displayed in the bottom right corner. At first, I wondered why the number 2 was there and noticed that nothing seemed to be happening. However, I soon realized that it was actually a loading icon. This icon eventually revealed a 3D simulation of what appeared to be the creator's studio.

Once the creative studio was visible, I hovered the mouse around to see the rooms move around with the mouse. Next, I clicked on Omada, which looked like a game room. Indeed, it was a 3D cartoonish sports room. I hovered over the car racing on the tracks, then clicked on it, and nothing happened. I clicked on the soccer ball and got coins for getting it in the goals. I clicked on the basketball and failed to get it in the hoop. I clicked on Discover Omada. Omada is a sports app; it's a social game that focuses on sports betting.
